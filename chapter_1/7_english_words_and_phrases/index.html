二 短语

  • be traced back to： 可以追溯到
  • a series of...: 一系列
  • plenty of...: 大量的
    • 比如 That farmer sold plenty of peaches yesterday. 那个农民昨天卖了很多很多桃子.
  • war of words: 争吵
  • single out: 单独指出，特别指出
  • 某个组织的领导:
    • 比如 head of the Australian Security Intelligence Organisation (Asio)
    • 也可以用 leader of ...
  • row with ... over ...: 和谁进行了争吵
    • 比如: row with China over Taiwan
  • spending spree: 消费热潮、消费盛宴
  • killing spree: 杀戮盛宴
  • exclusive news: 独家新闻
  • mind your own bussiness: 管好自己的事、少管闲事
  • do me a favor: 帮我个忙
    • do yourself a favor: 帮你自己一个忙. Do yourself a favor and get this movie on weekend
  • suffers from ...: 遭受...之苦
    • 比如: Japan often suffers from earthquakes. 日本经常遭受地震之苦.
  • in contrast to: 和...进行对比
    • 比如 In contrast to similar services in France and Germany, Intercity rolling stock is very rarely idle. 与法国和德国的类似铁路服务系统不同，“城际列车”很少闲置。
  • Robert took care of everything.
  • I prayed that no mother feels what I felt at the airport.
  • what would you do if you were in my place?
  • You should like you have a frog in your throat this morning.
    • 你今天早上听起来声音有点儿嘶哑。
  • You make being a parent feel easy.
  • your style of playing. 你的打法.
  • your hair style. 你的发型
  • In general, 总体来说
  • On the other hand, 另一方面来说
  • In simple terms, 简单来说

关于gold

  • “be as good as gold” 很乖，表现很好
  • “have a heart of gold” 有一颗金子般的心，非常善良和慷慨
    • She's always helpful, even when she's busy. She has a heart of gold.

关于bite

  • “bite your tongue” 忍住不说
    • 比如 I wanted to correct her grammar mistake, but I bit my tongue – it wasn't the right time.

  • “bite off more than you can chew” 贪多嚼不烂，承担力所不及的工作

    • I always bite off more than I can chew at work. I need to be more realistic with the number of projects I take on.
    • He agreed to plan the whole wedding by himself, but I think he's bitten off more than he can chew.

关于cake

  • “a piece of cake” 小菜一碟，非常容易的事
  • “have your cake and eat it too” 鱼和熊掌兼得，两全其美
    • 比如 You can't have your cake and eat it too – either save money or spend it on holidays.

  • “a slice of the cake” 可以得到的一份利益，分一杯羹

  • “the icing on the cake” 锦上添花或雪上加霜

关于foot

  • “best foot forward” 全力以赴，尽量做到最好

    • It's scary, but just put your best foot forward. You can do it!
    • Remember to put your best foot forward during the presentation tomorrow.

  • “sweep someone off their feet” 让某人神魂颠倒或倾心

    • He's taking you to a five-star hotel? Wow, he's really trying to sweep you off your feet.
    • Tom swept Sarah off her feet with surprise concert tickets and dinner at her favourite restaurant.

  • Put my foot down 表示坚决制止、坚决反对

    • 比如 I finally had to put my foot down when my roommate kept bringing friends over at midnight.
    • 比如 She's a little possessive, but I put my foot down when she gets too clingy. 她占有欲是有点强，但她太粘人时我会坚决反对。
    • 也就是当某事发生的时候，我进行了坚决制止； 而不能把整个短语作为一个动词, 比如制止某事: I put my foot down on sth. 这样是不能用的

关于时间

  • at dusk 在黄昏时分
  • at dawn 在黎明时分
  • at morning 早上

关于stop

  • “stop at nothing” 不惜一切代价

    • You're playing against Amina? Be careful, she'll stop at nothing to win.
    • We'll stop at nothing to solve this crime.

  • “put a stop to something” 停止（某事）；制止（某人的行为）

    • My manager keeps stealing my ideas. I need to put a stop to it.
    • I'm putting a stop to this party. It's already 2am!

  • “stop you dead in your tracks” （由于惊讶或刺激）令某人的行动猛然停止

    • When I heard the news, it stopped me dead in my tracks!
    • While the message from Mary is shocking, he did not let it stop him dead in his tracks and quickly responded.

  • “stop and smell the roses” 放慢节奏，享受生活

    • You're always too busy. You need to stop and smell the roses. Look at everything you have!
    • After working a long day, John chose to stop and smell the roses. He invited his neighbours to an impromptu garden party!

五 词根

1 ate

  • 表示: 被...的, 具有...的
  • 例如isolate, 可以分为 isol + ate
  • ate 也是一个动词后缀

2 isol

  • isol=isle
    • 意思来源于英伦三岛, British Isles, 其中Isle就代表岛的意思, 那么岛就是和大陆隔离的

3 clos 和 clud

  • 表示关闭

  • WC 缩写自Water Closet, 是指马桶后面那个水箱. 其中Closet就是那个箱子

  • 关闭: close

  • 不让你关闭、揭露、透露: disclose
  • include: 包含在里面
  • exclude: 不包含在里面
  • exclusive: 排他的、唯一的

4 circum

  • circum 和 circle（圆圈）相关

5 st

  • 是词根，代表stand、站立

6 ance

  • ance是名词后缀

7 co

  • co- 前缀，是com的简写，表示"共同、一起"
  • 也包含con,com

8 -ordin-

  • -ordin- 其实代表 order, 代表顺序、规则

9 ac、ar、at

  • ac-前缀是at的变体，是"朝向"、"去"、“to”
  • accuse就是"向内找原因"的意思，代表"控告、指责"

10 cuse

  • 是cause的简化版，代表"原因"

11 ex

  • ex前坠通常代表"向外"
    • 比如 excuse me: 打扰一下； 字面意思就是原因不在我、在外面
    • 比如 exit: 出口

12 de

  • de- 做前坠，代表"下"的意思, 也代表"消除"的意思

13 tain

  • tain代表take in, "拿住"的意思

    • 因此"detain"就是"拘留、留住、耽搁"的意思

14 re、rest

  • rest是"休息、终止"的意思
  • re是代表"回来"的意思
  • st是代表"站立"的意思
  • rest是"站回来"的意思，就代表"休息、终止"的意思

15 mon

  • mon 单个、一
    • 比如Monday, 比如Monarch

16 yard

  • "院子、场地"的意思，早期指"围场"
  • urt 代表 yard, yard 和 gard 也是近义词
    • 比如yard 约等于 garden (花园、菜园)
  • courtyard 是庭院的意思, A courtyard is an open area of ground which is surrounded by buildings or walls.
  • junkyard 是 废品场的意思, 等同于scrapyard

17 pol

  • 词根pol代表"光滑"的意思

18 pot 和 pos

  • 词根pot和pos都代表"能力"
    • 例如possible 和 imposible

19 re

  • 词根: 代表“回”,"再次","again"的意思,

20 spon

  • 词根：代表“assurance, 保障”的意思
    • 比如sponsor, 比如respond

21 ple

  • 词根 ple 代表"full"的意思，"充满"的意思
    • 比如deplete是耗尽的意思
    • 比如complete是完成的意思
    • 比如replenish是使再充满的意思

22 sequ

  • 词根 代表“跟随”的意思

23 ambi

  • 词根，代表“2”的意思
    • 比如 ambiguous 模糊的，模棱两可的

24 se

  • 含义: separte 使分开

25 cur

  • 含义: cur = cure = care

    • secure 是安全的意思，也就是说我们不用担心，和担心分开的意思
    • accurate 是朝着关心的地方去的意思. 就是“精准”的意思
